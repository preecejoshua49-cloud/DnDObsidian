# `=this.file.name`
## Overview

Hanbury is a tiny rural village in [[Hanbury]], With a population of 25. It has a quiet, timeless charm, with roots stretching back to the [[1. Age of the First Concord|Age of the First Concord]].

**Key Locations**

- **The Vernon Arms Tavern** – The village’s gathering spot for travellers and locals alike.
    
- **Hanbury Road Fishing Pool** – A serene pond popular with anglers and fey visitors.
    
- **Courth Close** – A small cul-de-sac of homes for villagers.
    
- **DP Optics** – A master glassmaker’s workshop known for fine lenses and enchanted glass.
    

The village sits along winding roads and gentle streams, surrounded by fields and woodlands. Life here moves at a peaceful pace, and the village has changed little since the [[5. Third Age of Harmony|Third Age of Harmony]].
