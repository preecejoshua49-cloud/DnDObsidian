## Background 

The grain here used to be plentiful, but since the strange happenings in [[Hanbury Woods]] the grain now grows tall and thick but rotten on the inside.

## Additional Details 

- The grain grows unnaturally tall in uneven patches.
- Some stalks bend _toward_ the characters, subtly.
- Cicadas are silent.
- The air smells sweet — almost fermented.
