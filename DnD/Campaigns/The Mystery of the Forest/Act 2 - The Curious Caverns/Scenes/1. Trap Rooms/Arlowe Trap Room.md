
## Overview 

**Scene Type**: Encounter

**Setting**: Extradimensional

**Encounter Type**: Puzzle

**Opening Description:** “You step toward a shallow pool of dark sap. Its surface is perfectly still. Your reflection stares back at you… but it’s wrong. Antlers twist from your skull. Bark splits your skin. And it smiles — just a heartbeat before you do.”

## Participants 

[[Arlowe]]

## Challenge 

**Must say something vulnerable in character.**

Then roll:

**Persuasion DC 13**

Success:

- Pool drains.
- Exit revealed.

Fail:

- Reflection reaches out.
- Dex Save DC 12 or become **Restrained** for 1 round.
- Take **1d4 psychic damage**.

### Argue With It

**Deception DC 14**

Success:

- Reflection flickers and cracks.
- Escape.

Fail:

- Reflection copies them perfectly.
- Must make **Wis Save DC 12** or become **Frightened for 1 round**.

### Attack It (Hard Mode)

Reflection has:

- AC 12
- HP 10

When reduced to 0:

- It shatters.
- PC takes **1d6 psychic damage** as backlash.
- Exit opens.

## Rewards 

Gain Inspiration or Advantage on next Charisma check.
