- “You are not yourself.”

## Participants 

[[Naeris Silvergrove]]

## Challenge 

**Arcana DC 15**

Identify which root pulses with actual magic.

**Nature DC 15**

Notice one root has real sap flow; others are illusion.

**Perception DC 16**

See slight shimmer in illusion-roots.

### Cutting a Root

If they choose blindly:

- Roll d6 (1–4 wrong, 5–6 correct)

Wrong root:

- Take **2d6 psychic damage**
- Must succeed **Wis Save DC 14** or be Frightened for 1 round

Correct root:

- Illusion collapses, door opens

**If they attempt to listen carefully:**

**Insight DC 15**

- Realize correct root whispers in their own voice
- They can auto-succeed next cut

## Rewards 

Gain Advantage on next spell attack OR spell save DC target has disadvantage once.
