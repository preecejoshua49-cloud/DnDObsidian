## The Holy Symbol Reaction

When a divine character approaches:

- The holy symbol embedded in bark faintly warms.
- Not when they pray.
- Not when they cast.
- Only when they physically reach toward the decay.

No roll needed — just describe it.

## Challenge 

**Perception DC 12**

He notices faint etching below the visible line — packed with dirt and sap.

If he scrapes it clean with tools:

**Thieves’ Tools DC 11**

Success reveals the full message:

> “The forest sees before it bites,
> 
> devours the old,
> 
> burns what remains,
> 
> and from ash, life returns.”

Failure:

Tool slips — needle prick.

Take **1 piercing damage**.

### Correct Solution

**Solution Order**

The sentence gives the order:

Eye (sees)
Fang (bites)
Leaf (devours the old – nature reclaiming)
Flame (burns what remains)
Seed (life returns)

Correct sequence:

Eye → Fang → Leaf → Flame → Seed

**Mechanics (Level 2)**

Figuring It Out

Investigation DC 12

Understand the sentence implies order.

If he struggles:

INT check DC 11 for a nudge.

---

**Pressing Buttons**

Each correct press:

Soft green glow.

Wrong button:

Thorns lash out.
Dex Save DC 12
    Fail: 1d6 piercing
    Success: Half damage

Sequence resets on mistake.

---

Completion

After correct sequence:

Door opens.
