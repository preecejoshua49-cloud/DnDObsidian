
## Overview 

**Scene Type**: Encounter

**Setting**: Extradimensional

**Encounter Type**: Puzzle

**Opening Description:** “You slam into something soft — and it moves. The walls around you inhale… and exhale. Roots press against your ribs in a slow, crushing rhythm. The air smells like sap and earth. Something here is alive… and it’s holding you.”

## Participants 

[[Heimskr]]

## Challenge 

**Athletics DC 15** (Action)

- Success → Pod splits, escape.
- Fail → 1d4 bludgeoning and still Restrained.

**Perception DC 12**

Notice expansion before contraction.

**Insight DC 11 OR Investigation DC 11**

Realize you must push during the inhale.

**Athletics DC 12**

Timed shove → escape.

Fail → 1d4 damage but can try again next round.
