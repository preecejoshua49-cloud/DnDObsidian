### Read Aloud

> He takes them slowly.
> 
> One by one.
> 
> His trembling hands steady as he gathers the shards.
> 
> A faint, twisted smile creeps across his face.
> 
> He exhales.
> 
> “Oaths… promises… lies bound in steel and silver.”
> 
> His voice grows steadier.
> 
> “They cannot command me.”
> 
> He closes his fist tightly around the shards.
> 
> “Only tempt me.”
> 
> His eyes lift to meet yours.
> 
> “By my broken oath… I answer only myself.”

The fragments cut deep into his palm. He exhales one final time. Dead.
